FAQ

Common Questions

Everything you need to know before getting started.

How do I send you my data? ›
WhatsApp is the easiest — just send photos of receipts, a voice note explaining what you need, or share a file. We work with whatever you have.
Do I need any special software? ›
No. Everything is built in Excel or Google Sheets — tools you already have access to on your phone or laptop. No new subscriptions required.
How fast will I get my deliverable? ›
Most once-off projects are delivered within 24–48 hours of confirmation. Larger projects are scoped with a clear timeline before we begin.
Can you come to my business physically? ›
Yes — we offer physical IT support call-outs in the Pretoria area. Remote support via TeamViewer or AnyDesk is also available nationwide.
What payment methods do you accept? ›
EFT, cash, or SnapScan. A 50% deposit is required before starting any project above R500. Full payment on delivery for smaller tasks.
Can you build something more complex? ›
Absolutely. The listed prices are starting points. Send us your requirements on WhatsApp and we'll give you a custom quote for any project size.
